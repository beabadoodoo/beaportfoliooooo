NU Wait n Plate: Canteen Queueing System

An Android-based intelligent queuing and sales tracking solution for canteens.

Developed for Android platforms, "NU Wait n Plate" revolutionizes canteen operations by providing a robust queuing system. It dynamically generates random queue numbers for customers, offering both **dine-in** and **take-out** options to cater to various needs.

The system includes comprehensive **history tracking** of all orders and provides real-time **counters** for "ordered," "no-show," and "total sales." Operators can **call** customers with an audible sound, **recall** numbers if needed, and mark orders as **no-show** for efficient queue management and accurate sales reconciliation. This app streamlines service and enhances customer flow in busy canteen environments.
